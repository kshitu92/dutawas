## Services Provided

- Visa guidance and processing support
- Tourist visa information
- Travel advice related to Nepal
- General consular support for the Nepali community in Western Australia
- Information support for Australian and foreign nationals planning to travel to Nepal

## Jurisdiction

The Consulate of Nepal in Western Australia serves communities and applicants within **Western Australia (WA)**.

For embassy-level diplomatic matters in Australia, contact the Embassy of Nepal in Canberra:

- [au.nepalembassy.gov.np](https://au.nepalembassy.gov.np/)
