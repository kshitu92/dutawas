## Services Provided

- Tourist visa support and guidance
- E-passport application process guidance
- Lost and found passport support
- General consular support for Nepali nationals and the wider community in South Australia

## Jurisdiction

The Honorary Consulate of Nepal in South Australia serves applicants in **South Australia (SA)**.

For embassy-level diplomatic matters in Australia, contact the Embassy of Nepal in Canberra:

- [au.nepalembassy.gov.np](https://au.nepalembassy.gov.np/)

## Important Information

- For online visa applications, select **SOUTH AUSTRALIA** as the visa mission.
- Visa approvals are sent by email after processing.
- An additional surcharge may apply for urgent visa processing.
- Use the consulate contact page for latest updates before applying.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is there a Nepal consulate office in South Australia?
   - Yes. The Honorary Consulate of Nepal in South Australia serves from Adelaide.

2. Which visa mission should I choose in the online form?
   - Select **SOUTH AUSTRALIA**, which appears as the Honorary Consulate of Nepal South Australia.

3. How can I contact the South Australia consulate?
   - You can email adelaide@nepal-consulate.net.au or call +61 43 448 6000.

4. Do I need to visit the office to receive a visa?
   - The consulate states approved visas are emailed after approval.
