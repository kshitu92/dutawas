## Services Provided

- Visa Services
- Attestation/Verification
- Power of Attorney
- NRN ID Services
- Passport and general consular communication support

## Jurisdiction

The Embassy of Nepal in Canberra is Nepal's official diplomatic mission in Australia and provides embassy-level consular communication for Australia.

For New South Wales (including Sydney), applicants may also contact the Honorary Consulate office in Sydney for local guidance:

- [Consulate of Nepal to NSW](https://consulatenepal.org/)

## Important Information

- Use `consular.canberra@mofa.gov.np` for passport and consular matters.
- Use `eoncanberra@mofa.gov.np` for other official communication.
- Check the embassy website before applying, as requirements and notices can change.
- Published office hours are Monday-Friday, with separate morning and afternoon windows.

## Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Canberra the official Nepal mission in Australia?
   - Yes. The official website identifies the Embassy of Nepal in Canberra, Australia.

2. Which email should I use for passport matters?
   - Use `consular.canberra@mofa.gov.np`.

3. Which email should I use for non-consular communication?
   - Use `eoncanberra@mofa.gov.np`.

4. What are the standard office hours?
   - Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM.

5. How do I get Australian or New Zealand documents attested by the embassy?
   - Australian documents such as divorce certificates and birth certificates must first be authenticated by the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT) in Australia.
   - New Zealand documents must first be authenticated by the Department of Internal Affairs in New Zealand.
   - Submit the authenticated document, a request letter addressed to the embassy, and a copy of the applicant's visa, passport, or other photo ID.
   - The fee is AUD 75.
   - If sending by post, include a money order or bank cheque payable to "Embassy of Nepal, Canberra" and a self-addressed express trackable return envelope.
   - Processing time is around 7 to 10 working days after the embassy receives the documents by post.
   - For urgent cases, documents can be brought in person before 12:00 PM on an office day for same-day attestation.

6. What documents are required for Nepali driving licence attestation or verification?
   - Submit a duly filled application form, a copy of the Nepali driving licence or a print copy of the online record from the Department of Transport, Nepal, and copies of citizenship and passport.
   - The fee is AUD 75.
   - If sending by post, include a money order or bank cheque payable to "Embassy of Nepal, Canberra" and a self-addressed express trackable return envelope.
   - If an old driving licence does not appear in Nepal's online driving licence system, a verification letter from the Department of Transport or Transport Office, attested by the Department of Consular Services in Kathmandu, is required.
   - If the driving licence details do not match the passport details, send an application letter explaining the discrepancy instead of the standard application form.
   - Processing time is around 7 to 10 working days after postal receipt, and urgent in-person submissions before 12:00 PM may be attested the same day.

7. How does bank guarantee or similar document attestation work?
   - For a bank guarantee letter, the applicant should attend the embassy in person with the required documents.
   - Required documents include the bank guarantee papers issued by the concerned bank and copies of the guarantor's citizenship, passport, and visa.
   - The bank guarantee attestation fee is AUD 150.
   - "To Whom It May Concern" letters, consent letters, and similar documents are charged AUD 75.
   - Seals and signatures on documents issued by Government of Nepal offices and certified or attested by the Department of Consular Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs can also be attested for AUD 75.
   - If documents are sent by post, payment may be made by money order or bank draft payable to "Embassy of Nepal, Canberra".

8. What are the submission times and postal address for attestation-related documents?
   - Document submission time is 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM.
   - Collection time is 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM.
   - Postal address:
     Embassy of Nepal
     PO Box No. 1070
     Mawson ACT 2607
