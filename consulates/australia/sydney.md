- Lost and Found Passport support
- General consular guidance for the Nepalese community in NSW

## Jurisdiction

The Honorary Consulate in Sydney serves **New South Wales (NSW)**.

According to its official announcement, Mr. Sanjeev Kumar Sharma was appointed as **Honorary Consul of Nepal for NSW, Australia**.

For embassy-level diplomatic and broader mission matters in Australia, contact the **Embassy of Nepal in Canberra**:

- consular.canberra@mofa.gov.np (Passport and other consular matters)
- eoncanberra@mofa.gov.np (All other communications)
- [au.nepalembassy.gov.np](https://au.nepalembassy.gov.np)

## Important Information

- Contact the Sydney consulate first for NSW-related consular guidance and service direction.
- Use the official service pages for the latest process details before applying.
- For mission-wide matters outside NSW honorary consular scope, use the Canberra embassy contacts.
- Public holiday closures may affect counter service hours.
